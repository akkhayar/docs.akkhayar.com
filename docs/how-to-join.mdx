# Joining Us

## Criteria

Applicant must:
- be a student of a [recognized university](#recoginized-universities).
- be a citizen of Myanmar.
- be able to communicate in English.

## Recoginized Universities

- GUSTO University, Yangon
- Device Business University, Yangon

Go to https://akkhayar.com/apply (or) click the button below to apply for a position as an internal contributor to the organization. You will be asked to fill out a form and submit a resume!
